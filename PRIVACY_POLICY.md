# Privacy Policy for RawNFC

**Last updated: February 2025**

## Overview

RawNFC is developed by VivoKey Technologies. This privacy policy explains how our application handles user data.

## Data Collection

RawNFC does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage information. The app operates entirely on-device.

## Data Storage

All NFC commands and responses are processed locally on your device. No data is saved to external servers or cloud services.

## Network Access

RawNFC does not require internet connectivity to function. The only network activity occurs when you choose to:
- Tap the VivoKey logo or app title to visit vivokey.com/rawnfc
- Use the share function to send NFC dump data via other apps

## Permissions

RawNFC requires only the NFC permission to communicate with NFC tags. No other device permissions are requested or used.

## Third-Party Services

RawNFC does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

RawNFC does not collect any data from any users, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
